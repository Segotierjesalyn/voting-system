🗺️ How to Vote — Step by Step

1

Create an account using your valid ID. Wait for admin approval before you can vote.
2

Use your email and password plus OTP verification to access the system.
3

Cast Your Vote
Select your candidates for each position. Review your ballot before final submission.

📜 Election Rules & Guidelines

  • ✅Each registered voter may only vote once per election.
  • ✅You must be at least 18 years old to register and vote.
  • ✅Your vote is secret and anonymous. No one can see who you voted for.
  • ✅Voting period is from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM on election day.
  • ❌Do not share your account credentials with anyone.
  • ❌Vote buying and selling is illegal and punishable by law.
  • ❌Do not take screenshots of your ballot. This is a violation of election law.
  • ❌Attempting to vote more than once is a criminal offense.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What if I forgot my password? ▼
Contact your barangay election officer or system administrator to reset your password. Do not share your credentials with anyone.
Can I change my vote after submitting? ▼
No. Once your ballot is submitted, it cannot be changed. Please review carefully before confirming your vote.
What is undervoting? ▼
Undervoting is when you do not select the maximum allowed candidates for a position. It is allowed — you are not required to fill all slots.
Is my vote really anonymous? ▼
Yes. The verification code only confirms that your vote was counted — it does not reveal who you voted for. Your choices are completely private.
What do I do if the system is down? ▼
Contact the election office immediately. The system will be restored as quickly as possible. Do not attempt to bypass security measures.
